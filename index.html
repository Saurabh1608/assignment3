About us

We are the Red Rants who have 3 words spouting from our think tanks. Benguluru, Football, United. We are an ever-growing prodigious 1500+ delirious United fans. MUFC-B can now rightfully be called a repository, a community, of United fans based in Benguluru. Our primary intent is to bring these hundreds of enthusiastic fans under the same roof by the dint of organizing screenings based on the Manchester United fixtures. MUSC-B tenaciously aims at conforming the soaring Red Flag in Benguluru.
